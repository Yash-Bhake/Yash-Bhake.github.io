About the Project

This research introduces a computational pipeline to analyze, capture and model the subtle melodic and metrical features that define expressiveness in Hindustani classical vocal music.

It's a genre known for its intricate structure and the beautiful, artistic deviations from its fixed compositional framework. Our work aims to model the artistic liberties that performers take to breathe life into a composition.

Why This Research Matters

Our goal is to create a deeper, quantitative understanding of musical performance and the strategies used by accomplished musicians.

Quantify Variability

Measure the degree of variability in timing and melody that indicates musical expressiveness.

Understand Strategy

Uncover the common patterns and strategies used by accomplished musicians during improvisation.

Build Generative Models

Contribute to models that can generate music from notation consistent with genre-specific intricacies.

Enable Comparison

Facilitate cross-cultural comparative studies of musical dynamics and improvisational grammar.
